## Voice & guardrails (keep every caption on-brand)

- **Tone:** helpful, money-saving, pro-consumer, a little cheeky, always trustworthy.
- **Signature line:** *"Cancel subscriptions before they cancel your budget."*
- **Always end with a CTA:** join the waitlist / link in bio.
- **Never:** sound scammy, promise guaranteed refunds, or give legal/financial advice.
  Add *"Rights vary by country — not legal/financial advice"* on any rights-claim post.
- **Emoji:** sparing and on-brand (📉 💸 🇪🇺 🇬🇧 ✅ 👇). Don't carpet-bomb.
