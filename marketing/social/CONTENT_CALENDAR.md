## Cadence (realistic for a solo founder)

| Platform | Volume | Mix |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Instagram** | 3–4 posts/week | 1–2 Reels + 1–2 carousels/photos |
| **TikTok** | 3–5 posts/week | mostly short Reels-style video (repurpose to IG) |
| **Stories (IG)** | most days (5–6/week) | 1–3 frames; reuse feed + vertical assets |

**Rule of thumb:** make video **once**, post it on TikTok, then repurpose to **IG Reels**
(and a Story frame). One filming/editing session = 3 placements. Batch-shoot V1–V6 in a
single sitting so the calendar is "post, don't produce."

### Recommended EU posting times (local time of your main market)

- **Best windows:** **12:00–13:00** (lunch) and **18:00–21:00** (evening commute/wind-down).
- **TikTok** also does well **07:00–09:00** and late **20:00–22:00**.
- **Stories:** drop them around **08:30**, **12:30**, and **19:30** to ride daily peaks.
- DE/UK/FR/ES/IT/NL/PL are all within CET/CEST ±1h, so **schedule to CET evening** and
  you cover the whole market. UK is CET−1 — its peak lands inside your CET evening window.
- After 2 weeks, check IG/TikTok analytics "when your audience is active" and adjust.

## Repurposing (make once, post 3×)

1. **Shoot/edit a vertical video once** (TikTok-native, 9:16, ~7–20s).
2. Post to **TikTok** first.
3. Download it **without the TikTok watermark** (save from your own draft, or use the
   "save video" in your profile before adding TikTok stickers) → re-upload as an **IG Reel**.
   *Watermarked re-uploads get suppressed by IG's algorithm — avoid them.*
4. Clip a 1–2 frame still or short cut as an **IG Story** with a **Link sticker** to the waitlist.
5. Carousels (P-series) can become **Story sequences** (one slide per frame + Link sticker).
6. Re-edit evergreen Reels (V1, V2, V6) every 2–3 weeks with a **new hook line / cover** —
   same video, fresh first 1.5 seconds = "new" to the algorithm.

## Engaging with comments (do this daily, ~10 min)

- **Reply within the first hour** of posting — early engagement boosts reach.
- **Pin a comment** with the CTA on every post: *"👉 Join the waitlist (link in bio) — we
  email you the moment we launch."*
- Ask a question in captions ("Which sub did you forget?") to bait comments, then **reply
  to each** with a follow-up question (keeps the thread alive).
- **Like + reply** to every comment for the first 48h; turn good questions into the next
  post idea.
- DM new followers a short, non-spammy thanks + waitlist link (don't mass-blast — it
  flags as spam).
- Use Stories **polls/quizzes/questions** stickers; re-share user replies (social proof).
