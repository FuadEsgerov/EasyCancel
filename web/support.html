How does cancelling work?

EasyCancel tracks each subscription's 14-day cooling-off deadline and generates a withdrawal/cancellation letter for you. Tap a subscription → Cancel now → Send cancellation letter to share it with the merchant (e.g. by email), then mark it as cancelled to keep a record in your Vault. EasyCancel does not contact the merchant automatically.

How do I add a subscription?

Tap the + on the Home screen to add one manually. You can also paste a confirmation email and EasyCancel will fill in the details for you.

How do I manage or cancel EasyCancel Pro?

EasyCancel Pro is billed through Apple. To manage or cancel it, open the iOS Settings app → your name → Subscriptions → EasyCancel, or use Restore purchases in the app's Settings.

How do I export or delete my data?

In the app: Settings → Export my data or Settings → Delete account. Deleting removes your account and all associated data.

Which countries are supported?

EasyCancel is built for the EU/UK right of withdrawal and is localised for English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Dutch and Polish.
