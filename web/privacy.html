Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026

EasyCancel ("the app") is operated by VINCLI LTD ("we", "us"). This policy explains what personal data we process and your rights. We designed EasyCancel to collect as little as possible and we do not use advertising or tracking.

Who is the data controller

VINCLI LTD is the data controller. For our company registration details, registered address, or any data-protection request, contact hello@vincli.com.

What we collect

  • Account data — if you sign in, your email address (or an Apple private-relay address). If you continue as a guest, no email is collected.
  • Subscription data you enter — merchant names, amounts, dates and cancellation records you add to track your subscriptions.
  • Preferences — your selected country and app settings.
  • A unique account identifier used to store and retrieve your data.

We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, and we do not include any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Why we process it (legal basis)

We process the above to provide the app's core features — tracking your subscriptions, alerting you to cooling-off deadlines, and generating cancellation/withdrawal letters. The legal basis is performance of our contract with you (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Where your data is stored

Your data is hosted on Supabase infrastructure located in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany). Supabase acts as our processor under a data processing agreement (DPA). Sign in with Apple and in-app purchases are handled by Apple under Apple's own privacy terms.

Retention

We keep your data until you delete it. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete account.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to the processing of your data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In the app you can:

  • Export your data — Settings → Export my data.
  • Delete your account — Settings → Delete account.

For any request, email hello@vincli.com.

Children

EasyCancel is not directed to children under 16 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
