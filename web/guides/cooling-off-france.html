Droit de rétractation: 14-day right to cancel in France

Subscribed online and changed your mind? French law gives you a droit de rétractation — 14 days to withdraw, no reason needed.

Withdrawal period
14 days
The law
Article L221-18, Code de la consommation

A subscription taken out online or by phone is a distance contract (contrat à distance). Under Article L221-18 du Code de la consommation, you have a droit de rétractation of 14 days to withdraw without justifying your decision and without penalty.

When does the period start?

For a service contract, the 14 days run from the day the contract is concluded. If the trader failed to inform you of the right, the period can be extended.

What's covered — and the exceptions (Article L221-28)

  • Digital content not on a physical medium: the right is lost if performance began with your express prior consent and you acknowledged losing the right.
  • Services fully performed before the end of the period, where you gave express prior consent, can fall outside the right.
  • If a service began at your request during the period, you may owe a proportionate amount for what was provided.

New for 2026: EU Directive 2023/2673 introduces a mandatory online "résilier mon contrat" button, applying from 19 June 2026 — alongside France's existing résiliation-en-trois-clics rules. Cancelling is becoming much simpler.

How to withdraw correctly

  1. Send a clear statement of withdrawal to the trader — email, the model form, or letter.
  2. Do it within 14 days and keep dated proof.
  3. Refunds are due within 14 days of your withdrawal.

Official source: Légifrance (Art. L221-18) · Help: INC.

Common questions

Does it apply after a free trial becomes paid?

The right attaches to the distance contract. Track the paid start date and act quickly — EasyCancel flags the deadline and the next charge.

What if I wasn't told about my right?

If the trader didn't inform you, the period can be extended by up to 12 months (Article L221-20). Keep your confirmation email.

Is rétractation the same as résiliation?

No. Rétractation unwinds a fresh contract within 14 days; résiliation ends an ongoing subscription going forward.
