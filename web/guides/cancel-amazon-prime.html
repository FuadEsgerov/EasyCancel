How to cancel Amazon Prime the right way

Cancelling Amazon Prime takes about a minute — but where you cancel depends on how you signed up. Here's how to stop the next charge and keep your benefits until your period ends.

First, check how you pay

This is the step most people miss. If you signed up on Amazon's website or app, cancel through your Amazon account. If you subscribed to Prime Video as a standalone subscription through Apple (the App Store handles billing), you must cancel in your iPhone settings — cancelling on Amazon won't stop an Apple charge. Note that full Amazon Prime (with shipping and other benefits) is managed only through Amazon, not Apple.

Cancel on the web (billed by Amazon)

  1. Go to amazon.com (or your local Amazon site) and sign in.
  2. Open the account menu and find Prime or Memberships & Subscriptions.
  3. Choose the option to end or cancel your membership and follow the prompts to confirm.
  4. Your benefits usually continue until the end of your current billing period. If you cancel and haven't used the benefits, Amazon may offer a refund in some cases — follow the on-screen options.

Cancel if you're billed through Apple (Prime Video)

  1. On your iPhone, open Settings and tap your name at the top.
  2. Tap Subscriptions.
  3. Tap Prime Video → Cancel Subscription.

Just signed up? If you only subscribed in the last 14 days in the EU or UK, you may have a cooling-off right that goes beyond simply stopping the next charge. Under EU and UK distance-selling rules you can usually withdraw from a new subscription within 14 days. See your country guide to check how it applies — and remember EasyCancel can track that deadline for you so it doesn't slip past.

Make sure it actually stopped

After cancelling, you should see a confirmation and an end date for your benefits. Watch your bank or App Store receipts next month to confirm no charge appears. EasyCancel can track the Amazon Prime renewal date and remind you before it bills again.

Common questions

Will I lose access immediately when I cancel?

Usually not. Amazon typically keeps your Prime benefits active until the end of the period you've already paid for, then they stop.

Can I get a refund?

If you haven't used your Prime benefits in the current period, Amazon sometimes offers a refund — the cancellation flow will tell you. Otherwise, the value is in cancelling before the next charge, which is what reminders are for.

I cancelled but was still charged. Why?

If you subscribed to Prime Video through Apple, cancelling on Amazon won't stop it. Cancel again under iPhone Settings › Subscriptions, then contact Apple about the charge.
