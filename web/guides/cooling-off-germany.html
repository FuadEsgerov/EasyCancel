Widerrufsrecht: your 14-day right to cancel in Germany

Signed up to a subscription online? German law gives you a Widerrufsrecht — a 14-day right to withdraw, no reason needed.

Withdrawal period
14 days
The law
§ 355 BGB

When you conclude a subscription online or by phone, it's a distance contract (Fernabsatzvertrag). Under § 355 BGB, together with §§ 312g and 356 BGB, you have a Widerrufsrecht — the right to withdraw within 14 days without giving a reason.

When does the period start?

For a service or subscription, the 14 days generally run from the conclusion of the contract. If the trader didn't properly inform you of your withdrawal right, the period can be extended.

What's covered — and the exceptions

  • Digital content (Streaming, Downloads): the right can end if you expressly agreed that supply begins before the period ends and confirmed you'd lose your Widerrufsrecht (§ 356 BGB).
  • Services already fully performed with your prior consent can also fall outside the right.
  • If a service started with your consent, the trader may charge a proportionate amount for what was delivered before withdrawal.

New for 2026: EU Directive 2023/2673 adds a mandatory online "cancel" button (Kündigungsbutton-style), applying from 19 June 2026 — on top of Germany's existing Kündigungsbutton rules. Leaving a subscription is getting simpler.

How to withdraw correctly

  1. Send a clear declaration of withdrawal (Widerruf) to the trader — email or letter.
  2. Do it within 14 days and keep a dated copy.
  3. Refunds are due within 14 days of your declaration.

Official source: gesetze-im-internet.de (§ 355 BGB) · Help: Verbraucherzentrale.

Common questions

Does Widerrufsrecht apply after a free trial converts?

The withdrawal right attaches to the distance contract. Track the paid start date and act fast — EasyCancel flags the deadline and the next charge.

What if the trader didn't tell me about my right?

If you weren't properly informed, the period can be extended (up to 12 months and 14 days under § 356 BGB). Keep the sign-up confirmation as evidence.

Is withdrawal the same as Kündigung?

No. Widerruf unwinds a fresh contract within 14 days; Kündigung ends an ongoing contract for the future. Use the one that fits your timing.
