Prawo odstąpienia: 14-day right to cancel in Poland

Subscribed online and changed your mind? Polish law gives you a prawo odstąpienia od umowy — 14 days to withdraw without giving a reason.

Withdrawal period
14 days
The law
Art. 27, Ustawa o prawach konsumenta

A subscription taken out online or by phone is a distance contract (umowa zawarta na odległość). Under Article 27 of the Ustawa o prawach konsumenta (the consumer rights act of 30 May 2014), you have a prawo odstąpienia — 14 days to withdraw without giving a reason and without cost.

When does the period start?

For a service contract, the 14 days run from the conclusion of the contract. If you weren't informed of the right, the period can be extended by up to 12 months.

What's covered — and the exceptions (Art. 38)

  • Digital content not on a physical medium: the right is lost if performance began with your express prior consent and acknowledgment that you'd lose it.
  • Services fully performed with your prior express consent can fall outside the right.
  • If a service started at your request during the period, you may owe a proportionate amount.

New for 2026: EU Directive 2023/2673 introduces a mandatory online "anuluj / zrezygnuj" button, applying from 19 June 2026. Leaving an unwanted subscription is getting much easier.

How to withdraw correctly

  1. Send a clear statement of withdrawal (oświadczenie o odstąpieniu) to the trader — email, the model form, or letter.
  2. Do it within 14 days and keep dated proof.
  3. Refunds are due within 14 days of your withdrawal.

Official source: ISAP (Sejm) · Help: UOKiK.

Common questions

Does it apply after a free trial converts to paid?

The right attaches to the distance contract. Track the paid start date and act quickly — EasyCancel flags the deadline and the next charge.

What if I wasn't told about my right?

If the trader didn't inform you, the withdrawal period can be extended by up to 12 months. Keep your confirmation email.

Is odstąpienie the same as wypowiedzenie?

No. Odstąpienie unwinds a fresh contract within 14 days; wypowiedzenie ends an ongoing subscription for the future.
